# LinkedIn Post: October 2025 Production Data

American whisky production fell 25% in October 2025.

Only 17.6 million proof gallons produced, down from 23.6 million in October 2024.

The producer count dropped too: 420 distilleries reported whisky production, down from 513 a year ago.

That's 93 fewer active whisky producers in one year.

The breakdown for October 2025:
- Whisky: 17.6M PG (-25% YoY)
- Rum/Gin/Vodka: 0.55M PG (+32% YoY)
- Brandy: 0.19M PG (-47% YoY)

Total spirits production rose 6.4% YoY, but that number is 99% industrial alcohol and neutral spirits.

Beverage spirits are contracting.

Source: TTB Distilled Spirits Statistics

# LinkedIn Post: 2024 Annual Summary

The American spirits industry produced 286.5 million proof gallons of whisky in 2024.

That's down 4.2% from 2023. The first decline in six years.

The five-year trend:
- 2020: 221.7M PG
- 2021: 242.2M PG (+9%)
- 2022: 278.5M PG (+15%)
- 2023: 298.9M PG (+7%)
- 2024: 286.5M PG (-4%)

Producer count also declined: 1,071 distilleries reported whisky production in 2024, down from 1,122 in 2023.

Other beverage categories fared worse:
- Brandy: -18%
- Rum/Gin/Vodka: -38%

After a decade of expansion, the American spirits industry is consolidating.

Source: TTB Distilled Spirits Statistics
