How It Works

Daily COLA monitoring that surfaces lead signals automatically.

Every beverage company files with the TTB. Before a new distillery ships their first bottle, before a winery launches a new label, before a brewery expands their lineup - they file a COLA. That filing is public data. The problem? It's buried in a clunky government portal that's impossible to monitor.

We pull new approvals daily, normalize the messy company names and categories, and classify each filing: is this a brand new company? A new brand from an existing player? A line extension? That classification tells you who's worth calling.

You get the full toolkit: a searchable database with 2M+ records, weekly reports by category, watchlist alerts when specific brands or companies file, and CSV exports. Spot a new distillery in your report, Google them, add to your CRM, make the call before your competitors know they exist.

Why This Matters for Lead Gen

  • Fresh leads daily: New companies need services. You see them first.
  • Signal, not noise: We filter 2,400 filings/week down to what matters.
  • Competitive edge: Your competitors are waiting for trade shows. You're not.
